Sponsorship Opportunities

Why Sponsor Our Event?

  • Promote your brand to a diverse audience.
  • Gain visibility through our marketing channels.
  • Network with potential clients and partners.
  • Receive recognition on event materials.
  • Get exclusive access to event data and insights.
  • Participate in post-event promotional opportunities.
  • Showcase your products or services during the event.

Rules for Sponsorship

  • All sponsorship applications must be submitted at least 14 days before the event.
  • Each sponsor is required to provide promotional materials two weeks prior to the event.
  • All sponsorships are subject to approval by the event organizers.
  • Sponsors are expected to adhere to event policies and guidelines.
  • Payment must be completed before the event date to secure sponsorship benefits.
